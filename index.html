About Me

I am now a Tenure-track Assistant Professor (Ph.D. Advisor) in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University. Also a member of MIPL Group (led by Prof. Yuxin Peng) at Peking University. I received my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof.Ying Wu from Northwestern University in 2018. Before coming to Northwestern, I have received my B.E. degree in the Automation Department from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in 2013. Before joining Peking University, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Assistant Professor working with Prof.Ying Wu in Northwestern University.
